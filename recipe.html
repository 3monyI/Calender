Alfredo Spaghtti Recipe

Equipment:

    • Measuring cup
    • 3 pans
    • Oven
    • 9X13 baking dish
    • Fork
    • Mixing Utensils

Ingredients:

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 yellow onion diced
  • 1tbs minced garlic
  • Seasoning of choice
  • 1 24oz jar pasta sauce
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • Garlic Bread
  • 1tsp pepper
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 2 cups parmesan cheese
    • 1 Pound Spaghetti 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven tp 350F
  2. Cook 1lb of pasta in salted boiling water according to package instructions
  3. While pasta is cooking in a seperate pot add ground beef, onion, and minced garlic, then season with seasoning of choice
  4. Once cooked through add in pasta sauce. Set to a simmer and set aside. Stirring occasionally.
  5. In a different pan on low heat add butter and heavy cream. Add in pepper and garlic powder.
  6. After seasoning is stirred in add parmesan cheese. Cook for 2-3 minutes until it starts to thicken
  7. To a 9X13 baking dish combine spaghetti and alfredo sauce. Por meat sauce on top and evenly distribute. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes uncovered, serve with garlic bread and enjoy
